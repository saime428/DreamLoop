Privacy contract

Dream text is written to a project-local `.dreamloop/` directory. DeepSeek and OpenAI are opt-in; Ollama keeps analysis on your own machine.

  • API keys stay in `.dreamloop/secrets.env`
  • designed to be forked, scripted, and extended
